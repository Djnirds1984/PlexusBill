## 🚀 Quick Start

### Step 1: Message Our Page
Open Facebook and send a message to our official page: **[Your Facebook Page Name]**

### Step 2: Register Your Account
Type:

### 💳 **Make Payments**

We offer **two convenient payment methods**:

#### Option 1: Online Payment (Instant)
**Command:** `PAY ONLINE`

- Pay using credit/debit card, GCash, Maya, or bank transfer
- Instant payment confirmation
- Automatic service activation
- Official receipt via Messenger

#### Option 2: Manual GCash Payment
**Command:** `PAY MANUAL`

- Send payment to our GCash number
- Upload screenshot of transaction
- Admin will verify and activate your service
- Confirmation via Messenger

**Quick Pay Command:** `PAY`
- Shows both payment options
- Choose your preferred method

## 🔐 Security Features

### **Account Protection**
- Each Facebook account can only link to **one** PPPoE account
- Your account information is only visible to your linked Facebook account

You can always re-register later with the same or different Facebook account.

## 🆘 Need Help?

### **Bot Not Responding?**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try sending `HELP` to see if bot is online
3. Wait a few seconds and try again

### **Registered Wrong Account?**
Simply type `REGISTER` again with the correct account number. The bot will automatically unlink the old account.

### **Payment Issues?**
- **Online payment failed?** Try again or use `PAY MANUAL`
